## Writing tips
- Do not start a sentence with a symbol.
- Avoid "Clearly" "Obviously" to start a sentence.


### Align

$$
\begin{align}
G &\equiv E + PV - TS \\
dG &\equiv TdS - PdV 
\end{align}
$$

```md
$$
\begin{align}
G &\equiv E + PV - TS \\
E &\equiv TdS - PdV 
\end{align}
$$
```
